# Distinguish provenance, integrity, authenticity, and completeness

Treat a provenance claim, transfer integrity, source authenticity, and source-set completeness as separate assertions. A URL, repository, revision, checksum, or signature proves only the property it actually verifies; do not infer authenticity or completeness from transport integrity alone.
